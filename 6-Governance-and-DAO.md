6. Governance & DAO Structure**

**Introduction**

The governance model of **WhalesBlock** is designed to ensure
**transparency, participation, and autonomy**.\
Through the **DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization)**, every
community member can actively contribute to the project[']{dir="rtl"}s
development, propose initiatives, and vote on key decisions shaping the
future of the ecosystem.

Governance is not an accessory element but one of
WhalesBlock[']{dir="rtl"}s **core pillars** --- a system where **the
project[']{dir="rtl"}s value and direction are determined
collectively**, not by a central entity.

**DAO Objectives**

**Decentralize Decision-Making**\
All major choices --- from treasury management to partnership strategy
--- are discussed and voted on by the community.

**Reward Active Participation**\
Users contributing ideas, time, or resources gain direct benefits in
terms of governance power, visibility, and rewards.

**Ensure Transparency and Sustainability**\
Every DAO operation is tracked on-chain.\
Transactions, allocations, and proposals are public and verifiable by
anyone.

**DAO Treasury**

The DAO treasury is the **economic heart** of the project.\
Managed through a **multi-signature wallet** and publicly verifiable, it
collects funds from:

-   NFT royalties (secondary markets)

-   Staking or internal transaction fees

-   Primary mint revenue

-   Scheduled \$WLOCK buybacks

-   Donations and partnerships

**Treasury Utilization**

-   Funding marketing campaigns and cross-chain collaborations

-   Airdrops and community rewards

-   Technical development and platform maintenance

-   Liquidity provisioning and management

-   Artistic and social initiatives tied to the WhalesBlock brand

All treasury spending must be approved through a **DAO vote**, requiring
a minimum **51% quorum**.

**Governance Evolution**

The WhalesBlock governance model is **evolutionary**:\
as the community grows, the DAO will progressively implement advanced
mechanisms such as:

**On-chain Smart Proposals** (automated proposal execution)

**Voting-as-Staking** (rewarding active voters)

**Delegated Voting** (temporary representation rights)

The goal is to build an increasingly **autonomous, transparent, and
community-driven** structure where members truly shape the
project[']{dir="rtl"}s direction.
