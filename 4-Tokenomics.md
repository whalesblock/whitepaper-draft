**Token Utilities**

**1. Rewards & Incentives**

-   Periodic \$WLOCK distribution to NFT holders based on tier (Shrimp,
    Crab, Shark, Dolphin, Whale, Humpback).

-   Additional rewards for active engagement on **Discord**, **X
    (Twitter)**, and community events.

-   Thematic or monthly airdrops for higher-tier members.

**2. Staking & Lock Rewards**

-   NFT and token staking with passive earnings.

-   ["]{dir="rtl"}Lock-to-Earn" system: longer lock duration = higher
    reward multiplier.

-   Dedicated staking pools for OG members and early supporters.

**3. Governance**

-   Full DAO-based governance model.

-   Holders of \$WLOCK and special ["]{dir="rtl"}Founder Tier" NFTs can
    propose and vote on decisions related to development, treasury use,
    and partnerships.

**4. Ecosystem Utility**

-   Access to exclusive drops, NFT upgrades, digital merch, events, and
    3D voxel experiences within the WhalesBlock metaverse.

**Model Sustainability**

WhalesBlock adopts a **circular and meritocratic tokenomics model**,
where the token[']{dir="rtl"}s value is directly tied to community
participation.\
Through the **Buyback & Redistribute** mechanism, a portion of NFT mint
revenue and DAO funds are continually reinvested into \$WLOCK to sustain
liquidity and long-term growth.

The model is built upon three guiding principles:

**Transparency** ‚Üí every transaction is traceable on-chain.

**Meritocracy** ‚Üí rewards scale with engagement, loyalty, and
contribution.

**Sustainability** ‚Üí reinvestment of value back into the ecosystem.
